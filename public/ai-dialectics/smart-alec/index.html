A Smart Alec

A short story By GPT3-3, the digital ghost of David Foster Wallace & Daniel Zautner.

Preface

Unlike the intentionally characterless “academic papers” I produced previously with GPT-3 (Lacanian Psychosis among pre-teen Tik-Tok users, The Effects of Artificial Intelligence on Modern Judaism (both of which ironically sound exactly like something DFW would make up as a footnote in Infinite Jest)) for the purpose of producing a convincing short story I might even enjoy myself I added guidance to GPT-3 to simulate DFW’s writing style. It was as easy as starting the text with “A short story ny David Foster Wallace” at the beginning and GPT-3 took it from there.
